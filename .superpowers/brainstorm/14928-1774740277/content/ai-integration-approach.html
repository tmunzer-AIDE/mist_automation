C. Integrated Expandable Card

The "AI Analysis" toggle sits inline with the chips. When clicked, an analysis card expands between the chips and the diff list with a smooth animation. The trigger button becomes part of the page's existing visual language. Most integrated feel — the AI is part of the data, not a separate layer.

Key Difference

A = separate collapsible row. B = side panel (layout shift). C = button is inline with chips, analysis expands below as part of the same card flow. C is the most "native" feel and easiest to reuse since it's just a trigger button + expandable section.
